About Us

I started this blog to document my life after college, mostly to share the recipes I baked on the weekends. I was in my mid-twenties, working in finance, and felt a little lost in both my career and my life. I tapped into my many, many, MANY memories being in the kitchen when I was younger, picked up a camera (taught myself how to use it!), and the rest is history.
